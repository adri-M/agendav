.. _calendar:

Calendar interface
==================

Views
-----

AgenDAV offers four calendar views, switchable from the buttons in the top
right corner:

- **Month** - full month overview, events shown as short bars
- **Week** - seven-day agenda with hourly time slots
- **Day** - single-day agenda with hourly time slots
- **List** - plain list of upcoming events over a configurable number of days

Navigate between periods with the **prev/next** arrows or click **Today** to
jump to the current date. You can also click directly on a date number in the
month view to jump to the day view for that date.

Creating events
---------------

**Click on a time slot** - click any empty slot in the week or day view to
open the event editor pre-filled with that time. In month view, click on any
day to open the editor for that day.

**Drag across time slots** - in the week or day view, click and drag downward
across multiple time slots to create an event that spans exactly the time you
selected. The editor opens with the start and end times pre-filled.

**Drag across days** - in the month view, click and drag across multiple days
to create a multi-day all-day event.

Editing events
--------------

**Click an event** to open a details popover with a quick summary. From there
you can open the full editor or delete the event.

In the full editor you can change the title, calendar, time, description,
reminders, and recurrence settings.

Moving events
-------------

**Drag and drop** an event to a new time slot or day to move it. The change is
saved immediately without opening the editor.

- In the week or day view, drag to any time slot.
- In the month view, drag to any day.
- Dragging between a timed slot and the all-day row (or vice versa) converts
  the event between timed and all-day.

To undo a move, press **Ctrl+Z** in the browser immediately after - the page
must not have been reloaded.

Resizing events
---------------

In the week or day view, drag the **bottom edge** of an event up or down to
shorten or extend it. The new end time is saved immediately.

Calendars sidebar
-----------------

The left sidebar lists all your calendars and any delegated or subscribed
calendars. Click the coloured dot next to a calendar to show or hide its
events. Click the calendar name to open its settings (colour, display name,
sharing options).

The **+** button at the top of the sidebar creates a new calendar or adds an
iCal feed subscription (if enabled by the administrator).
